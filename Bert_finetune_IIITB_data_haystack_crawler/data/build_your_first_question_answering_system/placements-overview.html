IIITB RANKED NO.1 TECHNICAL UNIVERSITY (PRIVATE)

Set up in the year 1998, and promoted by the Karnataka Government and the IT Industry, IIIT Bangalore attracts quality students every year from all over India and abroad, to its acclaimed Integrated M.Tech, M.Tech, MSc in Digital Society and Research Programmes. The institute has a distinguished history of producing high quality post-graduate engineers every year.

The 3600+ strong alumni occupy key positions in a number of corporations, delivering tremendous value to their organizations. They have been recruited in wide range sectors like BSFI, Product, IT Services, HFTs, research labs and have assumed diversified positions ranging from SDEs, Member of Technical Staff, Technology Analyst, Technology Consultant in all levels of seniority. Students from different technical and cultural backgrounds come together in the institute, realizing the potential of unity and teamwork. Immense support from the wonderful faculty enables the students to stay on the path of scientific enquiry and together, they have produced several patents, published in influential journals and conferences and carried out innovative projects.

We are happy to share that most of the sought after companies recruit from IIIT Bangalore. Thanks to all the industry and faculty support, most of our students are placed by the time they graduate.

The programmes and specializations that IIIT Bangalore offers

  • M.Tech in Computer Science (CSE)
  1. Theory and Systems for Computing and Data
  2. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  3. Networking and Communication
  4. Cyber Security
    ​
  • M.Tech in Electronics and Communication (ECE)       
  1. VLSI Systems
  2. Networking and Communication
  3. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

(The focus areas of the VLSI specialization include Digital & Analog VLSI Design, System Design with FPGA, Functional Verification of SoCs and Design for Testability (DFT), Real Time Operating Systems, Inter Device Communication, Device Driver Development, Firmware and ARM Architecture. Other topics covered in Mtech ECE include High Level Synthesis, Advanced Analog Design and ASIC flow.)

  • Integrated M.Tech. (iMtech) Programme (CSE and ECE)  
  1. Theory and Systems for Computing and Data
  2. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  3. Networking and Communication
  4. Cyber Security
  5. VLSI Systems
  • M.Sc. in Digital society        
  1. Human-Centered Digital Design
  2. Data Intensive Digital Design
  3. Research and Policy studies

(This course makes the students congnizant of the current technologies and practices, thereby equipping them with the tools and knowledge to solve contemporary real problems. The students gain insights into the design of digital technologies, and the policy challenges of deploying such technologies, with a broad-based training that will draw from computer science, research methods, human centered design, human computer interaction, management, economics and other social sciences.)

  • MS(Research)/PhD
  1. Networking, Communications & Signal Processing (NCSP)
  2. RADAR Sensing
  3. Data Science
  4. Computer Science
  5. VLSI and Embedded Systems

(This program provides a transformative academic experience for mature students to expand knowledge and generate novel insights by investigating complex issues with cutting-edge methodologies. Few of the focus areas of this program are Beam management in 5G/6G networks, Channel estimation in IRS systems,  Physical-layer Security, Fundamentals of RADAR, Applications of AI/ML in RADAR sensing , AI and Big Data, Algorithms, Cryptography, Formal verification of machine learning algorithms and applications).

Why Hire from IIIT Bangalore?

  • The admissions for MTech and Integrated MTech are based on GATE Scores and JEE Main Ranking respectively. The admissions for Research Programmes and MSc in Digital Society are based on Institute tests and rigorous rounds of Interviews.
  • The course curriculum is designed in such a way that it gives freedom to select a lot of courses provided in various specialization pools. The curriculum is based on the latest industry standards and is updated yearly to match the same. Apart from core subjects, students are also awarded specializations upon completing a minimum number of prerequisite courses. The rigorous curriculum allows the students to interact and collaborate with other disciplines which evolves them holistically.
  • All our academic programmes gear the students to face the corporate world in companies that are engaged in advanced R&D, products and services. 
  • The research conducted at IIIT Bangalore along with academic programmes also provide a firm foundation for motivated students to pursue research by enrolling in PhD programmes in leading universities in India and abroad.
  • Our faculty members have a Ph.D. with distinguished research and teaching experience in leading institutions around the world, due to which the teaching quality is world-class. The professors are always ready to help you out in your research projects, thesis, and course curriculum. The course curriculum is very well designed that offers its students a wide range of courses to choose from as per his/her interest areas, which makes its students ready to work for in the competitive industry.

 

Important Queries

  • Timeline of Internship and Full Time Employment – Please refer to the timeline tab.
  • What is the recruitment process and timeline?
    • August for dual offers (Internship plus full time) or only full time
    • September/ October for only 6 months’ internships
    • November / December for summer internships
    • January for 11 months’ internships (VLSI)
  • What options do students their final semester?
    Internship / Thesis / Exchange Programme / Start up with the IIITB Innovation Center / Research Projects with Faculty members. (It is mandatory to complete at least 5.5 months of the above as on June 15th each year in order for the students to graduate.)
  • What is the placement policy? 
    We follow a One job per student policy (MTech / iMTech Threshold – INR 8LPA and MSc in Digital Society – INR 6LPA). Please refer to the guidelines for details information. 
  • What is the CGPA cutoff?
    CGPA is out of 4 and the passing cut off is 2.4.
  • How do students get a specialization?
    Students need to complete 5 courses in the respective area to get a specialization in that domain. 
  • What are the expectations from companies providing internship?
    The institute will collect midterm feedback around March to understand the progress of the students and help them improve where needed. It is also needed for grading them. At the end of the internship / thesis / exchange programme / start up - students have to submit a report and completion certificate, latest by June 20th each year. This is an important milestone in order for the students to be graded and graduate.
